# 🏆 Kianmehr Gold Trading Platform

Kianmehr Gold Trading Platform is a web-based gold trading and jewelry management system designed to provide a simple, secure, and scalable experience for customers and administrators.

The platform supports jewelry sales, coin trading, melted gold transactions, wallet management, article publishing, and financial operations through a modern modular monolithic architecture.

---

## 🎯 Project Vision

The goal of this platform is to provide a reliable and user-friendly environment for managing gold-related transactions while maintaining security, performance, and future scalability.

The system enables:

- Jewelry product management
- Coin purchasing and selling
- Melted gold trading
- Wallet management
- Withdrawal requests
- Financial transaction tracking
- Content and article management
- Administrative management dashboard
